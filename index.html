FEEDBACK

Unfortunately, a significant number of children today are unaware of their legal rights, and this lack of knowledge can potentially lead to troubling situations. Without a basic understanding of their rights, children may find themselves vulnerable to exploitation, discrimination, or unjust treatment. Educating children about their rights not only empowers them to protect themselves but also ensures a brighter and more equitable future for all young individuals.

Little Voice is an innovative and engaging website designed to educate children and young adolescents about their legal rights through a captivating gamified platform. In an increasingly complex world, understanding one's rights is essential, and this website strives to make this learning experience fun, interactive, and educational.The platform is intended for children between the ages of 8 and 16.
